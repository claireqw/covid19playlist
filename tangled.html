"When Will My Life Begin"

“When Will My Life Begin” from Tangled is the story of a princess who has been imprisoned in a tower for her whole life. Brought up by many as a lighthearted example of what life is like in shelter-in-place, the song details the tasks Rapunzel does to pass her day and her longing to go out. It captures the frustration and boredom of having to stay at home all day, and the feeling of looking for something more, that many are feeling during the quarantine.
