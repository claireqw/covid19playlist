“All I Ask of You”

“All I Ask of You” is a piece from Phantom of the Opera. Andrew Llyod Webster, the composer for the musical, posted a video of him playing the piano version of the song, and the orchestra for the musical responded to it by individually sending in videos of them playing the piece and compiled to make a virtual orchestra. The video is an example of how musicians have had to adapt to the pandemic; now that they can no longer rehearse or hold performances in person, many have had to seek new ways of spreading their music.
