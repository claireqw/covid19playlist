“Wishing You Were Somehow Here Again”

“Wishing You Were Somehow Here Again” is another piece from Phantom of the Opera. This recording is from the 2011 performance at the Royal Albert Hall, celebrating the 25th anniversary of the musical, and posted on The Shows Must Go On!. Andrew Llyod Webster started this channel during the pandemic, uploading recordings of full length musicals for 48 hours, to keep the public entertained and encourage donations to the organization The Actors Fund. In this song, Christine misses her late father and seeks guidance, and her emotion resonates with those who are separated from loved ones during this pandemic.
