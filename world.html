"If the World Was Ending"

“If the World Was Ending” was originally written about the earthquakes in Los Angeles a year ago, but the artists thought it also applied to the COVID-19 pandemic. Emphasizing the importance of love and compassion, especially for people that you’ve lost connection with recently, JP Saxe and Julia Michaels invited friends in the music industry to record them singing from their homes, reiterating the importance of connection. They encouraged donations to the Doctors with Borders by fans watching the video, and the proceeds from the video will also be going to the organization.
